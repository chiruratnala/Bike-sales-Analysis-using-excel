Bike Sales Data Analysis
========================

📊 Dashboard
------------


📌 Project Overview
-------------------

This project provides a detailed analysis of **global bike sales data** using Microsoft Excel. The dataset contains over **112,000 sales records** covering multiple countries, product categories, and customer demographics.

The goal of this project is to convert raw transactional data into a **clean, interactive dashboard** that helps identify sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

This dashboard helps businesses understand **which products sell the most, which regions generate the highest revenue, and how customer demographics influence sales.**

🎯 Business Problem
-------------------

To improve sales strategy and product planning, businesses need insights such as:

*   **Product Performance:** Which bike categories and products generate the highest revenue?
    
*   **Regional Sales:** Which countries and states contribute the most to total sales?
    
*   **Customer Demographics:** How do factors like **age group and gender** affect purchasing behavior?
    
*   **Profitability:** Which products and categories produce the highest profit margins?

📈 Analysis & Insights
----------------------

### 1️⃣ Revenue Distribution

**Bikes** generate the highest revenue compared to accessories and clothing due to higher product prices.

### 2️⃣ Regional Performance

The **United States and Australia** contribute a large share of total bike sales.

### 3️⃣ Customer Demographics

The **Adults (35–64)** age group represents the largest customer segment purchasing bike-related products.

### 4️⃣ Product Categories

Three main categories dominate the sales data:

*   🚴 Bikes
    
*   🧰 Accessories
    
*   👕 Clothing
    

Accessories have **high sales volume**, while bikes generate **higher revenue per sale**.

### 5️⃣ Profitability

High-end bikes and specialized accessories produce the **highest profit margins** in the dataset.

🚀 How to Use
-------------

1.  **Download** the Sales Analysis.xlsx file from this repository.
    
2.  **Open** the file using **Microsoft Excel (2016 or later recommended)**.
    
3.  Use the **Slicers** to filter data by:
    
    *   Year
        
    *   Country
        
    *   Product Category
        
    *   Customer Gender
        
4.  View the **KPI cards** to quickly understand:
    
    *   Total Revenue
        
    *   Total Profit
        
    *   Total Orders
        
    *   Total Sales Quantity
        
5.  Explore the charts to identify **sales trends and customer insights**.
